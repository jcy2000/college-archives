Our Story

In 2022, Gang Green was founded with a simple mission, build a cool website that you'd use to decide whether a Steam game was worth buying

When our team was recently formed, we had a vision. A vision to create a cool website. We appreciated the reviews that people left on games, and we wanted to give other people easy access to it.

But the knowledge at the time was very limited - Code would break (it still does at times), links wouldn't work, and the website format would get a little messy. Not only was it a major blockade for us, but it also limited our growth as a team. And that frustration led us to a solution.
